Welcome to the Network

One donation can save up to three lives. Here's everything you need to know before your first donation.

How it Works
1

Complete Registration

Fill in your personal details, blood type, and location to join the donor network.

2

Get Matched to Requests

When a compatible patient needs blood nearby, you'll receive an urgent alert.

3

Go to the Hospital

Confirm your availability and head to the hospital. Staff will guide you through the rest.

4

Track Your Impact

Your donation history and eligibility status are tracked in your profile.

Basic Eligibility
Requirements
Age 18–65 years old
Weight at least 50 kg (110 lbs)
In good general health
No recent fever or illness
No donations in the last 3 months
No tattoos or piercings in last 6 months
💡

Tip: Stay hydrated and have a light meal before donating. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before your appointment.
